The Situation in Jokkmokk, Sweden

It has been noticed that the new church in Jokkmokk has become popular on social media. We are happy to announce that to visit the new church, you must pay 10 dollars to get in and out if you are not a resident. In addition, you must pay 5 dollars for each hour you spend in the new church. This way we ensure that everyone gets a fair price.

Also, the tax will bump the 10 dollar fee to 20 dollars in the future. This is because of tax. Taxes are to help schools, roads, and other things alike. This way the normal things in the USA are avalible.

The experence is going to be great for people alike. We hope that tourists appreciate this law and will be understanding. Kind regards, Government of Sweden.
